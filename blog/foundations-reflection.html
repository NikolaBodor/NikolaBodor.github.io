The Roles of Values, Empathy and Self-Awareness in Learning and Programming?

The role of values in learning is to help with giving you a purpose and in turn more motivation to learn and explore new things, in programming is to help you write ethical and useful code making sure it's used for it's intended purpose.

The role of empathy in programming and learning is that it improves collaboration with your peers and helps you communicate and understand them better. Empathy also helps you programme solutions which will solve the issue because you'll understand their needs and limitations

The role of self self-awareness in programming and learning is to help with improving yourself and your abilities in programming and learning, it shows your weaknesses, strengths and helps with identifying good learning methods for you and with setting goals and asking for help when needed. Self-awareness also helps to realise when you have reached your limitations and helps you manage your time and stress better.
