EQ and IQ

16/09/25


Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence is about the ability to indentify and manage your own emotions as well as recognise and understand the emotions of people around you.

EQ vs IQ

IQ refers to mainly cognitive abilities like processing, reasoning, memory and analytical skills while EQ refers mainly to understanding and manageing your own emotions, building interpersonal relationships with others and empathy.

Importance of EQ

Having a higher EQ leads to having better conflict resolution, being more resiliant in stressful circumstances and more willing to colaborate and contribute. These are very important skills to have in any workplace.
